How to enter the reaction

You enter the reaction as [element index](element)[element coefficient]+[another element]=[resulting elements], you need to enter the reaction without spaces. Because of elements like Aluminum (Al), or Chlorine (Cl), capitalization is important. A few examples of correctly entered reactions are:

CH4+O2=CO2+H2O
Al+O2=AlO
NH3+NO=N2+H2O
CON2H4=C3N6H6+CO2+NH3
